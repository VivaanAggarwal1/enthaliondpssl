Enthalion

“Where Curiosity Reacts”

Started by the students of Delhi Public School Sushant Lok, Enthalion is a paradox in motion — part lab, part rebellion, entirely curiosity. It lives in the space between precision and impulse, between the formula and the spark that ignores it. Structured enough to measure, reckless enough to matter. It’s not about experiments; it’s about the people who can’t stop making them.

Our Purpose

Enthalion is where curiosity stops asking why and starts proving it wrong. We mix, burn, and break things (safely) to uncover what textbooks only hint at — the real reactions, the real chaos, and the real science that doesn’t wait for permission.

What: Discover Science Beyond Textbooks

We design and perform live experiments that connect theory with reality—no rote learning, just raw observation and logic.

Why: Science should be seen, not studied.

Enthalion makes curiosity dangerous and ideas inevitable. If it can be done on paper alone, it doesn’t belong here.
