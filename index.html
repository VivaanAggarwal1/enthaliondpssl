About Enthalion

What the club actually does.

Our Vision

Turn textbook science into real experiments, projects, and competitions. We learn by building, not just memorising.

Who Can Join

Open to all classes and streams. No minimum marks, only requirement: curiosity and willingness to show up.

What We Do

  • Hands-on experiments and demos
  • Interschool Olympiads and quizzes
  • Project building and exhibitions
  • Talks by teachers and alumni
