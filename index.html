ENTHALION
Science Club
“where curiosity reacts”

Started by the students of Delhi Public School Sushant Lok, Enthalion is a paradox in motion — part lab, part rebellion, entirely curiosity. It lives in the space between precision and impulse, between the formula and the spark that ignores it. Structured enough to measure, reckless enough to matter. It’s not about experiments; it’s about the people who can’t stop making them.
